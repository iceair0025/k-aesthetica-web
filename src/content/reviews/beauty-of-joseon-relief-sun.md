I've returned three "holy grail" sunscreens for pilling or white cast. This one sinks in like a light serum, leaves a soft glow (not greasy), and plays nice under makeup. Combination skin here — no breakouts after six weeks of daily use. The only catch: it's a chemical filter, so give it a few minutes to set before going outside.
