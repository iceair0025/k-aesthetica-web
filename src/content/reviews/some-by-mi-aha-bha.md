Oily, breakout-prone skin. Used three nights a week, it genuinely helped with clogged pores and texture on my forehead. But the name is doing a lot of work — this is an active exfoliant, not a gentle "toner," and using it nightly wrecked my barrier for a week. Three stars because the results are real but the marketing sets people up to overuse it. Start twice a week, max.
