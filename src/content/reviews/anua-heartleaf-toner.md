Sensitive, easily-flushed skin here. This is the first toner in years that didn't sting. Lightweight, no fragrance drama, and the redness around my nose genuinely settled down within about a week of morning-and-night use. I pat two layers in on damp skin. It won't transform your skin, but as a calm, no-drama hydration step it's earned a permanent spot.
